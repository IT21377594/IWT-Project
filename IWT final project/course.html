Courses We Offer

Our degree programs provides students with the opportunity to complete a degree via a number of different routes

Integrated masters programmes

Our integrated masters programmes are undergraduate programmes that contain an extra year of study at postgraduate level. They are designed for students who are ready to make the jump from school or college to university, or for individuals who are looking for a career move.

Postgraduate research degrees

Study for a PhD, research masters or professional doctorate at the University of SLBM. University of SLBM offers a range of postgraduate research degrees across all areas of our world-leading research.

Professional development

Professional development is an ongoing process which helps you understand your working environment, the job you do and how to do it better.

Our Global Campus

Obtain a career-focused education with a globally recognised university while simultaneously preparing for a successful career.
